This folder contains present, observed data for environmental layers such as photosynthetically active radiation (PAR), pH, salinity, sea surface temperature (SST) and current velocities (UVEL) in .tif files.


Data for PAR, salinity, SST, and UVEL are from Bio-Oracle: Marine data layers for ecological modelling.

Data for pH are from Global Marine Environmental Datasets for Species Distribution Modelling and Environment Visualisation.


Sources:


Assis, J., Tyberghein, L., Bosh, S., Verbruggen, H., Serrão, E. A., & De Clerck, O. (2017). Bio-ORACLE v2.0: Extending marine data layers for bioclimatic modelling. Global Ecology and Biogeography.


Basher, Z., Bowden, D. A., Costello, M. J., 2018. GMED: Global Marine Environment Datasets for environment visualisation and species distribution modelling. Earth Syst. Sci. Data Discuss., https://doi.org/10.5194/essd-2018-64. Accessed at http://gmed.auckland.ac.nz on <Access DATE>


Tyberghein L, Verbruggen H, Pauly K, Troupin C, Mineur F, De Clerck O (2012) Bio-ORACLE: A global environmental dataset for marine species distribution modelling. Global Ecology and Biogeography, 21, 272–281.
